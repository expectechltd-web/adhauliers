About Us

Your Trusted
Haulage Partner

AD Hauliers Ltd is a Glasgow-based premier haulage service provider with over three years of industry experience. We specialise in delivering efficient transportation solutions tailored to the diverse needs of businesses across various sectors.

As a registered UK limited company (SC811338), we operate on a national scale — ensuring your goods are transported safely and promptly to any destination within the United Kingdom.

Our team is driven by a passion for client relationships and backed by GPS tracking, telematics, and rigorous driver training to ensure every delivery is a promise kept.
