About Us

Your Trusted
Haulage Partner

AD Hauliers Ltd is a premier haulage service provider with over three years of industry experience, operating from two strategic centres — Glasgow, Scotland and Bolton, England — giving us excellent coverage across both the north and the rest of the UK.

As a registered UK limited company (SC811338) with an operating licence covering the UK and Europe, we ensure your goods are transported safely and promptly to any destination — domestic or international.

Our team is driven by a passion for client relationships and backed by GPS tracking, telematics, and rigorous driver training to ensure every delivery is a promise kept.
